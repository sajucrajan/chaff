Pick how aggressively to chaff the AI. Higher intensity = more AI friction, more candidate load.
Pick N of 10 Low
Just your 10 real questions, numbered. You say "answer 7." AI has to solve 10 in parallel to keep up.
Shuffle & reveal Medium
Screen starts with 10 nonsense questions built from your vocabulary. Press Reveal — words fly to their correct positions, forming the real questions. Dramatic, AI-hostile.
Flicker periphery Medium
10 real questions, with flickering fragments around the edges that poison every AI screenshot.
Full chaff High
Shuffle & reveal + flickering fragments around the edges. Maximum AI friction. Recommended only for senior/adversarial roles.
OCR poison Maximum
Words flicker invisible 10×/sec. A human reads normally; Tesseract.js-based AI tools get a different incomplete fragment every screenshot. Defeats real-time OCR pipelines.
